Are you licensed to run these activities?

Yes, we are fully licenced and all our instructors have been given full training for running these activities.

How old must my child be to participate in one of these activities?

We have an age range of 8 years and above, although some activities are labelled as having a different range. Height and weight restrictions may also apply.

Can I contact my child from offsite?

Yes, we have several telephones around the site. Please refer to the 'Contact Us' page for contact details, including telephone numbers.
